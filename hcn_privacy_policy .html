1. What Information Do We Collect?

Personal Information You Disclose to Us

In Short: We collect personal information that you provide to us.

We collect personal information you voluntarily provide when you register on the Services, express interest in our products, participate in activities, or contact us. The personal information depends on your interactions with us, your choices, and the features you use, and may include:

  • Names
  • Phone numbers
  • Email addresses
  • Contact or authentication data (e.g., Google Sign-In credentials)

Sensitive Information. We do not process sensitive personal information, such as racial or ethnic origins, sexual orientation, or religious beliefs.

Payment Data. If you make purchases, we collect data necessary to process payments (e.g., payment instrument number, security code) via our payment processor, Razorpay. We do not store sensitive payment information on our servers. All payment data is handled by Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant gateway. See their Privacy Policy.

Social Media Login Data. If you register using social media accounts (e.g., Google, Facebook, X), we collect profile information like name, email address, and profile picture, as described in Section 5.

Application Data. If you use our app, we may collect, with your permission:

  • Geolocation Information. We request access to your device’s location (precise latitude and longitude) for the “Discover Nearby Doctors” feature, only with your explicit consent via an in-app disclosure and system permission prompt. You can disable location access in your device settings, but this may limit location-based features.
  • Camera. We request camera access solely for scanning QR codes, with your permission. You can manage this in your device settings.
  • Mobile Device Data. We collect device information (e.g., device ID, model, manufacturer, OS version, IP address) for security, troubleshooting, and analytics.
  • Push Notifications. We may request to send push notifications for account or app updates. You can opt out in your device settings.

All personal information you provide must be true, complete, and accurate, and you must notify us of changes.

Information Automatically Collected

In Short: Some information, like IP address and device characteristics, is collected automatically.

We automatically collect information such as IP address, browser/device characteristics, OS, language preferences, referring URLs, and usage data (e.g., timestamps, pages viewed, features used) for security, analytics, and reporting. This data does not reveal your specific identity.

Location Data. We collect precise or imprecise location data (via GPS, Wi-Fi, or cell towers) for location-based services like “Discover Nearby Doctors.” You can opt out by disabling location settings, but this may limit functionality.

Camera. We use camera access only for QR code scanning, with your permission.

Google APIs. Our use of Google APIs (e.g., Google Maps for location services) adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including Limited Use requirements.

2. How Do We Process Your Information?

In Short: We process your information to provide, improve, and administer our Services, communicate with you, ensure security, and comply with law.

We process your information for:

  • Account Management. To facilitate account creation, authentication, and management.
  • Service Delivery. To provide features like finding nearby doctors or processing payments.
  • User Support. To respond to inquiries and resolve issues.
  • Administrative Communication. To send updates about our products, terms, or policies.
  • Order Fulfillment. To manage orders, payments, or returns via Razorpay.
  • User Communication. To enable user-to-user interactions (e.g., messaging doctors).
  • Feedback. To request feedback on our Services.
  • Marketing. To send promotional communications (with consent; opt-out available).
  • Security. To prevent fraud and ensure Service safety.
  • Analytics. To evaluate usage trends and improve Services.
  • Legal Compliance. To comply with legal obligations or defend our rights.

3. When and With Whom Do We Share Your Personal Information?

In Short: We share information in specific situations with third-party service providers.

We share data with third-party vendors who perform services for us, with contracts to safeguard your information. These include:

  • Cloud Computing Services (e.g., Firebase)
  • Data Analytics Services (e.g., Firebase Analytics)
  • Payment Processors (e.g., Razorpay)
  • User Authentication Services (e.g., Google Sign-In, Firebase Authentication)
  • Google Maps Platform APIs (for location services)

Other Situations:

  • Business Transfers. We may share data during mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales.
  • Other Users. Information you share in public areas (e.g., profile data via social login) may be visible to others.
  • Legal Requirements. We may disclose data to comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data or share it for advertising purposes.

4. Do We Use Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies?

In Short: We may use cookies and tracking technologies for analytics and security.

We use cookies, web beacons, and pixels to collect usage data, maintain security, prevent crashes, and save preferences. Third parties (e.g., Firebase Analytics) may use tracking technologies for analytics. See our Cookie Notice for details.

5. How Do We Handle Your Social Logins?

In Short: Social logins provide us with profile information.

If you register via social media (e.g., Google, Facebook, X), we receive profile information like name, email, and profile picture. This is used only for account creation and authentication. Review the social media provider’s privacy policy for their data practices.

6. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

In Short: We keep your information only as long as necessary.

We retain personal information for as long as needed for the purposes in this policy or as required by law (e.g., tax, accounting). Account data is kept while you have an active account. When no longer needed, we delete or anonymize data, except for data in backup archives, which is securely stored until deletion is possible.

7. How Do We Keep Your Information Safe?

In Short: We use technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

We implement security measures like encryption (e.g., HTTPS, Firebase protocols), access controls, and regular audits. However, no system is 100% secure, and transmission of data is at your own risk. Use our Services in a secure environment.

8. Do We Collect Information from Minors?

In Short: We do not collect data from users under 18.

We do not knowingly collect or sell data from users under 18. If we learn such data was collected, we will deactivate the account and delete the data. Contact us at hcnofficial161@gmail.com if you believe we have minor data.

9. What Are Your Privacy Rights?

In Short: You can review, change, or delete your account data.

You have rights under laws like GDPR or CCPA, including:

  • Access. Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction. Correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion. Request account deletion.
  • Withdraw Consent. Revoke consent for data processing (e.g., location, notifications).
  • Opt-Out of Marketing. Unsubscribe from marketing emails via the unsubscribe link or by contacting us.

To exercise these rights, contact us at hcnofficial161@gmail.com or use the in-app “Delete Account” feature under Settings > Account.
